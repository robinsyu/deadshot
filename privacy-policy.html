Introduction

Welcome to the Deadshot Privacy Policy. At Deadshot, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at deadshot.top and play our game.

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our game and website, including:

  • Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, username, or similar identifiers when you register to play Deadshot.
  • Technical data including IP address, browser type and version, time zone setting, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform, and other technology on the devices you use to access this website.
  • Gameplay data including scores, achievements, and in-game performance statistics.
  • User-generated content that you provide in your communications with us or other users.

How We Collect Your Information

We collect information through:

  • Direct interactions when you provide us with your details by filling in forms or corresponding with us.

We may use the information we collect about you for various purposes, including to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Provide and maintain our service, including to monitor the usage of our service.
  • Improve our website and gaming experience.
  • Communicate with you, including sending you updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Administer and protect our business and website.
  • Deliver relevant website content and advertisements to you.
  • Measure or understand the effectiveness of the advertising we serve to you.

Disclosure of Your Information

We may disclose your personal information in the following situations:

  • To our service providers and business partners to perform services on our behalf.

  • The right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you.

  • The right to data portability to receive a copy of the data we have on you in a structured, machine-readable format.
